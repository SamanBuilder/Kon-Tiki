Frequently Asked Questions

I didn’t receive the verification email

Check your spam/junk folder first. If it’s not there, email support and include the address you used to sign in.

How do rankings work?

Rankings are based on your performance in quizzes and multiplayer matches (ELO-style). Stronger performance increases your rating.

How can I delete my account?

Email support and request account deletion. Include your username and the sign-in method used (Google or email).

Report a bug

When reporting a bug, include your device model, iOS version, and what you did right before the issue happened.
